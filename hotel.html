Kathmandu Yaman's Hotels & Resorts

Short Description

Designed a luxury hotel and resort website focused on premium visual storytelling, elegant layouts, and a smooth hospitality browsing experience..

Project Information

  • Role: UI/UX Designer
  • Tools: Figma
  • Platform: Website
  • Duration: 1 week
  • Style: Luxury / Modern Hospitality
  • Type: Hotel & Resort Website

Problem Statement

Many hotel websites lack visual consistency and fail to create a premium first impression, making it difficult for users to explore services, rooms, and amenities comfortably.

Proposed Solution

I designed a modern hospitality website with immersive visuals, organized content sections, elegant typography, and user-friendly layouts to improve the browsing and booking experiences.

Design Process

  • Hospitality website research
  • Wireframing Planning
  • Visual UI Design
  • Layout structuring
  • Section Based Information Hierarchy

Section flow

Hero Section

Introduces the hotel brand with booking navigation, search controls, and luxury visual presentation.

Welcome section

Highlights the hotel’s identity, hospitality experience, and premium service introduction.

History section

Presents the hotel’s background story and brand heritage through structured content layout.

Journey Showcase

Displays travel destinations and featured experiences using modern visual cards.

Awards & Recognition

Highlights hotel achievements and hospitality awards to build trust and credibility.

Rooms & Suites

Showcases room categories, amenities, and luxury accommodation details.

Gallery Section

Provides immersive resort visuals and lifestyle-focused imagery for engagement.

Amenities Section

Displays hotel facilities and guest services in a clean icon-based layout.

Dining Section

Highlights food services and restaurant experience through featured content blocks.

FAQ & Location

Helps users access common information and hotel location details quickly.

Contact & Booking Section

Provides inquiry forms, booking details, and contact information in a structured layout.

Key Design Decisions

  • Used luxury-inspired dark blue and gold color palette
  • Focused on spacious section layouts for readability
  • Added immersive resort imagery for stronger visual engagement
  • Maintained consistent typography and spacing system
  • Designed section based storytelling for bettter user flow

Outcome

This project demonstrates my ability to design premium hospitality website with strong visual hierarchy, modern layout, and engaging user experiences tailored for luxury hotel brands.
